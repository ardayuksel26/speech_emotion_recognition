\subsection{Example Equation}
Equations should be used to define mathematical models or algorithms. \autoref{eq:perlin_sample} demonstrates a simple function used in terrain generation.

\begin{equation}
    f(x, y) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \frac{1}{2^i} \text{noise}(2^i x, 2^i y)
    \label{eq:perlin_sample}
\end{equation}

Where $f(x, y)$ represents the altitude at coordinates $(x, y)$, and $n$ is the number of octaves.
